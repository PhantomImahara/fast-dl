Quark - Capture The Flag
By Ziggo

Objective: Enter the enemy Base and make
your way to where their Flag resides. Grab it and bring it
back to the middle bunker or the top of your Base. 
Place it on the raised square to Capture it. 

Scoring: 10 points per Capture on the middle bunker.
15 points per Capture on the top of your Base.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
