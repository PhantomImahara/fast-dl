====================================
PC Zone BattleGround
====================================

For 1-on-1, or 2-on-2 battles.

Notes:
-Once you leave your resupply room, you
can't get back in. The only other resupply 
pack is in the middle of the map.

-To spectate, hit one of the spectating
booths. Hit it again to stop.

-Play fair! No respawn camping allowed!
