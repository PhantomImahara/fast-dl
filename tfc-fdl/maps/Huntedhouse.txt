The Hunted House

OBJECTIVE:
The Hunted must survive long enough
to reach the escape truck alive.
It is waiting for him in the rear 
driveway.

Either aid in his escape or
attempt his murder.

IF THE HUNTED LIVES:
His team earns 50 points.

IF THE HUNTED DIES:
The Assassins earn 50 points.
The Informant earns 25 points.
