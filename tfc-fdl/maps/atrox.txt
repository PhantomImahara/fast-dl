Atrox Reportare (Hard-fought Victory)
True Capture the Flag || by A Boojum Snark

==Make your way to the enemy control center and
turn off the security. Grab the flag and bring it
back to where YOUR flag resides to capture.
Your flag MUST be home to capture, if not, wait
for it to return.

After a flag is captured everything is reset and
a new round will begin.

==Scoring per capture:
10 team points.
8 points to the capper.
2 points to everyone else on your team.

==Dropped flags are returned:
after 50 seconds.
if it is touched, on the enemy side.

==The flag locater only detects YOUR flag when it 
is being carried on the ENEMY side.
