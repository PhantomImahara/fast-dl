============================================
                POINTE DU HOC 

              Invade or defend
============================================

          by Bryan "Zouave" Dillow

Objective: Blue team must take their flag 
through the Red team's defenses to win the  
game.  There are three command points on the 
way which must be secured in order.

Scoring: Blue team - 4 points per minute
	 Red team - 30 points for capturing 
			the beach
		  - 30 points for capturing 
			the farms
		  - 40 points for capturing 	
			the town

Other Notes: 

- Dropped flags return after 75 seconds
- When a capture point is secured, 
spawn points are revised and a new flag 
appears. 
- Rounds last 25 minutes.
- Flag carriers move at 75% of normal speed
