DOMINATION - Terratorial command
By UncleBRUCE

Primary objective: Place a flag from 
your flag room onto each of the five 
command points.

Secondary objective: Destroy the enemy 
base. Open the enemy detonation point 
and place your detonation key upon it.

Scoring: 1 point for every 30 seconds
you hold a command point. 10 point 
bonus for destroying the enemy base.

Other Notes: Flag carriers move at 
half-speed. Detonation point remains 
open FOR 60 SECONDS ONLY!
