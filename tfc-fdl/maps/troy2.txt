Troy2 - Invade and defend

The Attackers must capture all FOUR command points 
in sequence to take the city of Troy.

The Defenders must defend the command points 
in the city of Troy against the Attackers.

When all the command points in the city of Troy 
are captured, the city is lost and a new round 
begins.  The attackersbecome the defenders and 
the defenders must now attack the city.

Scoring: Attackers - 10 points per command point, 
	 Defenders - 1 point every 15 seconds defending
