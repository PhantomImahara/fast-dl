Horizon - King of the Hill
by User Err0r (dongollwitzer@gmail.com)

Story:
Two enemy nations at war in the Middle East
are fighting for control over a strategically
placed bunker.

Objective:
Take your teams data card to the control
station in the bunker to capture it for your
team.

Scoring:
2 frags and 5 points per capture.
2 point every 15 seconds the bunker is under
your team's control.

Notes:
Detonate the enemy computer console for points
and to make them lose control of the bunker.
A teleport will open up in your base when your
team has control of the bunker. There is a 15
second delay before the teleport reactivates
after being used.
