Boom Arena v2 by Krypt-Tonix

Objective: Take the soccerball located
in the center of the map and carry it
to the enemies base.  Either cap it on the 
platform or leave a detpack in the 
computer room for team points.

Scoring: 10 points per capture.
	5 ponts per computer det.

Other Notes: Command Center Displays 
show who has the ball and where it is.
