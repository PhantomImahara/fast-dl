OverLoad

by mk_ultra/parc


Capture The Flag


Objective:

Pick up the enemy Flag and 
take it to your Capture Point.


Scoring: 

10 Points per Flag Capture


Other Notes: 

Flag Carriers drop the Flag when they die. 
Dropped Flags return to their Base after 40 seconds.
Spies lose their disguise when they pick up the Flag.
Grenade Bags respawn after 50 seconds.
