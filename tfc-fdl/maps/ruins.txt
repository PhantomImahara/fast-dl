The Ruins - Invade and defend 
by Reaper 47
(reaper47@gmx.at)

Objective: Blue team (Attackers) must bring their 
flag to the Command Point inside the Red base.
Red team (Defenders) tries to defend the 
Command Point as long as possible. Once the 
Attackers succeed, the flag is reset and all 
players are moved back to their starting positions.

The gates open after 60 seconds. The flag returns
after 3 minutes.

Scoring:

Blue team - 15 points for reaching the Command Point
inside the Red base.
Red team - 10 points each time the enemy flag 
returns. Additionally points are awarded every 5 
minutes for defending the Command Point.
