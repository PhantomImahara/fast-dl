Alpine - Assault

Objective: Blue must penetrate the
Red mountain base, retrieve the flag,
and bring it back to their cave outpost.
If Blue can find a way to seal off the
Flag Turret, they will gain easy entree.

Falls from a height can be deadly.

Scoring: 
25 points if Blue captures.
10 points per minute that Red keeps
their flag.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 30 seconds.
