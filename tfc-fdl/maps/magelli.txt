Magelli - Invade and defend

Objective: Blue team starts as the attacker.
Red team starts as the defender. The attacking
team must secure 3 command points
to achieve victory.
The defending team must keep
the attackers from capturing
the command points.
Once the attacking team captures
all three command points,
the teams swap,
and it starts all over again.

Other notes:

Flag carrier moves at 85% speed
Dropped flags return after 30 seconds
Scoring:
Attacking team - 10 points per Command Point
captured.
Defending team - Points are awarded over time
for defending the command points.
