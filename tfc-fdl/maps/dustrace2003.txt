Dust Race 2003 by SpiKe

www.mallosworld.co.uk/creating_worlds

Objective: 
Race along the track, try and get to the
finish line first.

Scoring: 
10 points for the team and the person who
gets to the finishing line.

Notes:
If you fall off the cliff you go back to
the start line.
Go through the pitstop for an health and 
armour boost.
