No Mans Land 3 - Meatgrinder
By UncleBRUCE

Objective: Enter the enemy base and make 
your way to the basement where their flag
resides in the back of a truck. Grab it
and bring itback to your battlements. Place
it on the raised square to capture it.

Scoring: 10 points per capture. 10 frags to 
the capper.

Note: Flag carriers drop the flag when they 
die. Flags return to their original spot 90 
seconds after it has been dropped.
