Attack/Defend. Or Keep Away.

Notes:  
Flag returns immediatly.
Flag NOT ALLOWED in respawn area.  If you try to enter, 
the flag will be returned!  Flag resides at IL CENTRO!

Attackers, move through the village and steal
the flag.  Hold on to it for as long as possible.

Defenders, set up your defense and beat back the attackers!

Scoring:
Attackers 2 pts every 12 secs when you have the flag.
Defenders 2 pts every 24 secs when the flag is at its base.
