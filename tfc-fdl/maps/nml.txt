No Mans Land - Capture The Flag

Objective: Enter the enemy Base and make
your way to the lower level where their Flag 
resides in the enemy truck. Grab it and bring 
it back to your Battlements. Place it on the 
raised square to Capture it. 

Scoring: 10 points per Capture. 10 frags to
the player who captures it.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
