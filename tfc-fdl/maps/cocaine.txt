Cocaine - Kill The Drug Baron

By SpiKe www.mallosworld.co.uk/creating_worlds

Objective: Welcome to a Columbian Drug den.
Rival factions are at war for a large amount
of coke. The assassins must take out the drug
baron so they can monopolise the market.
The bodyguards must protect their boss so they
can get a cut of this month's sales.

Scoring: 5 pts every 30 seconds to bodyguards
for protecting the drug baron. 10 pts to 
assassins for killing him.
