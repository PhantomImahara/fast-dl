
Opposing Forces - Capture The Flag

By:Jay "Deride" Lindholm of the Kindred Clan.

Based On RIP's original TF version of Oppose.

Objective: Enter the Enemy Base and make
your way to where their Flag resides Upstairs.
Grab it and bring it back to your
Battlements. Jump it through the hole in the
ceiling to Capture. 

Scoring: 10 points per Capture.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
