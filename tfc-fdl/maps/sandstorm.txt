SandStorm - Domination

Objective: Take the Flags from your 
Base and put them on the Command 
Points situated in the map. Use 
the signs located around the map
to see who owns each Command Point.

Scoring: 1 point for every 30 seconds
you hold a Command Point.

Other Notes: 
*There are 3 Command Points
*Dropped flags return immediately
*When a team caps all 3 points, they
 get 25 extra points
