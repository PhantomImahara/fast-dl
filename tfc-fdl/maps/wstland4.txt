Map Description		: This is a standard 2 team, 2 flag TeamFortress Classic CTF level.
Map Objective		: You have to take the flag from the enemy base and place it it on the dropoff in your base. (the same place as you flag is kept)
Scoring Information		: 10 points per flag capture.  One for a kill
Number of Teams		: 2
Recommended # of Players	: 12 to 16
