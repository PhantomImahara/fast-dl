* Tips & Notes *

If you want the gluon or the tau guns, you have to pay for them
in some of your life!

Weapon/Ammo Count:
3 ammo_357
6 ammo_9mmAR
3 ammo_9mmClip
5 ARGrenades(x2)
4 ammo_Buckshot
5 ammo_Crossbow
5 ammo_Gaussclip
4 ammo_RPGClip
2 weapon_357
4 weapon_9mmAR
2 weapon_Crossbow
1 weapon_Egon
1 weapon_Gauss
4 weapon_Handgrenades
1 weapon_Hornetgun
1 weapon_RPG
4 weapon_Shotguns
2 weapon_Snarks
4 weapon_Tripmines

There are 8 info_player_deathmatch entities, 10 item_battery entities, 
10 item_healthkit entities, and 1 item_long_jump entity.

There is one secret area (not referring to the room with the snarks).
