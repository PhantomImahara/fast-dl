Included with this map is a POWER-UP Mario player model and a Luigi player model.
Use them to jump and run in the mario_air environment of the map.
Place the powerup_mario.mdl and the powerup_luigi.mdl in your valve/models/player folder.

Also included is a zipped folder containing a snark replacement model.  It is of the mushroom
koopa's which usually run around in Mario Levels.  If you want to use the snark replacement, you'll have to unzip it to it's proper locations which are laid out for you in the zipped readme.

INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip mario_air.zip.
Open the mario_air folder and place the mario_air.bsp, mario_air.res, and mario_air.txt
in your valve/maps folder.  Place the 6 (six) grass.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.
Place the 9 wav files in a folder called mario2 and place that in your valve/sound folder.
Place the spinning_coin.spr, cloud.spr, and explode1.spr in your valve/sprites folder.
Place the cannon_guy.mdl in your valve/models folder, and take the power_up Mario & Luigi player models and put them in folders inside the player folder which is inside the valve/models folder.
